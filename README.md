Title-
Samsung Mobile Market Analysis & Product Launch Strategy

---
Description-

This project analyzes the smartphone market across multiple brands to evaluate Samsung’s competitive position. Using web-scraped data, the dashboard identifies gaps in pricing, battery, and performance, and provides data-driven recommendations for new Samsung product launches.

---
Tech Stack-

* SQL – Data cleaning and transformation
* Power BI – Dashboard development and visualization
* DAX – KPI calculations and advanced metrics
* Web Scraping – Data collection from online sources

---
Data Source-

* Smartphone data collected via **web scraping** from online mobile listing platforms
* Includes data for 7 brands:
  Samsung, Oppo, Vivo, OnePlus, Xiaomi, Realme, Motorola

---
Features-

* Brand-wise comparison of **price, battery, RAM, camera, and spec score**
* Segment analysis (Budget, Mid-range, Premium, Flagship)
* KPI metrics:
  * Spec Score Gap
  * Battery Gap
  * Charging Gap
* Samsung vs Competitor benchmarking
* Matrix visualization** for feature comparison
* Recommended Samsung launch series** based on market gaps

---
Key Questions Solved-

* How does Samsung compare with competitors across key features?
* In which price segments is Samsung leading or lagging?
* What are the major feature gaps in Samsung’s lineup?
* What specifications and pricing should Samsung target for new launches?

---
Business Impact & Insights-

* Identified that Samsung **lags in battery capacity and fast charging** compared to competitors
* Found stronger positioning in **budget and mid-range segments**, but weaker in premium and flagship
* Proposed **4 new Samsung series launches** with optimized:

  * Pricing
  * Battery capacity
  * RAM and performance
  * Camera features
* Provides actionable insights for **product strategy and competitive positioning**

---
Project Outcome-

This project demonstrates end-to-end data analysis:

* Data collection → Cleaning → Analysis → Visualization → Business Recommendation
